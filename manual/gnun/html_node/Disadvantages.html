1.4 Known Bugs and Limitations

As it happens in real life, we don't wear pink glasses and are aware of certain limitations and annoyances of this semi-automatic system.

  • The official build is invoked thrice daily1, because doing it more often will potentially generate more messages to the mailing list in the form of commit notifications. This has its drawback, since translators have to wait 8 hours until their PO files are updated, and another period for the .lang.html articles to get generated, after they commit the updated POs. Additionally, any errors interrupt the build so they have to be fixed before the next articles are processed.

Footnotes

[1] The “no-grace” items are rebuilt hourly.
